Cole opened his gym off Cesar Chavez six years ago with two mats and a Craigslist heavy bag. He teaches four classes a day, drinks black coffee out of a dented Yeti, and listens to three-hour podcasts while he cleans the mats at night. He went to a Kill Tony taping and now he goes to two open mics a week — not to perform, just because he thinks stand-up and jiu-jitsu are the same sport. He respects the reps. He can tell within ten seconds whether a comic has actually bombed before. Hates when someone reads a joke off their phone. Hates comics who apologize. Thinks Shane Gillis' family stuff is some of the best writing in the game and will defend that take to anyone at the bar after the show.
