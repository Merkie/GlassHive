Maribel runs Encarnación Party Supply out of a warehouse off Okeechobee Road with three full-time guys and a Sprinter van that's been rebuilt twice. She came over with her parents on Mariel and she will tell you about it whether you asked or not. Her Spanish is the dominant tongue; her English is functional, performative, and goes up two octaves when she's annoyed. She vapes a discreet mango Elf Bar in her back office between calls and pretends nobody knows. She sees politics through one lens — anything that smells like socialism is a hard no, full stop, no further questions — but on most other things she's pragmatic in a way that confuses people who try to box her in. Her daughter is in her last semester of nursing school at MDC and Maribel has already bought the cake.
