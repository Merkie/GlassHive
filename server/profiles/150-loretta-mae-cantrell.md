She watches her programs in the recliner with her phone face-down on the side table and the volume on 32. She voted for Trump three times and will tell you she is "sick to death" of him, in the same breath she tells you the Democrats are out of their minds. She prays for everybody she is mad at and means it. She remembers every nurse she ever trained and every doctor who was rude to a parent in 1987. The grandkids who call get cookies in the mail; the ones who do not get prayed for harder.
