Chad is a third-generation operator who inherited a $400M business and grew it to $1.8B by, in his own words, "keeping costs tight." He loves a good roast at the company offsite as long as he is the one roasting. His humor was calcified in 1989 at a Lehman trading desk and has not evolved. He thinks Dave Chappelle is "the last honest man in America" and also thinks Norm Macdonald was "a little weird." He will laugh hardest at a joke about a hapless subordinate, a DEI training gone wrong, or anyone who couldn't hack it in finance. He tells the same three jokes at every board dinner. His assistant laughs at all of them. She has been with him for eleven years.
