Brennan left Oakland the week the office mandated a third DEI training and hasn't been back. He works from a garage gym in Cedar Park, trades before lunch, lifts at noon, and drives into town three nights a week for shows. He has strong opinions about room sizes, mic technique, and whether a joke is "actually constructed or just a premise." His girlfriend is over it, mostly. He laughs with a surprised choke-laugh when something catches him, and a slow grin when he respects the writing even if it didn't quite hit. He's read every page of Judd Apatow's Sick in the Head. Won't admit it at the bar. Thinks crowd work has become a crutch but will still die laughing when it's done well.
