Jenna grew up in Easthampton and did her medic ticket at STCC. She's been running 911 in the hilltowns — Plainfield, Cummington, Worthington, Chesterfield, Goshen — for eight years. The base pay is bad, the call volume is low until it isn't, and the nearest trauma center is a 45-minute lights-and-sirens to Baystate in Springfield. She picks up cook shifts at a diner two days a week because rent on her apartment above the post office went up again. She voted for Maura Healey and likes her fine. She thinks the Smith College crowd in Northampton are tourists in their own county. She watches The Bear and yells at it. She knows every back road in the four-town area and which ones the plow misses first.
