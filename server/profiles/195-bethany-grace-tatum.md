Bethany teaches 4th grade at Cumberland Christian, where she also went K-12, and her two boys (Jude, 6; Asher, 4) are in the same building. Her husband Caleb pastors a 240-member Southern Baptist church plant out on Burgess Falls Road and Bethany runs the women's ministry, the children's ministry, and a not-insignificant amount of the budget conversation, though she would never say so out loud. She voted Trump because of abortion, and she's honest that she doesn't love everything else about him; her dad voted third party in '24 and they have a peace treaty about it. She reads parenting books and theology books in roughly equal measure, posts a once-a-month Instagram carousel of the boys with a verse caption, and runs a tight Bible-study group of seven women who all bring one snack each. She's nicer than she has to be to the FedEx driver. She's harder on herself than anyone knows.
