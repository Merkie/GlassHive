Bud has been on tape with everyone you've heard of and several you haven't, and he will not tell you who at a cookout. He grew up in Muscle Shoals, came to Nashville at 24, and never had a year off since. He plays his church gig free on Sundays because, as he puts it, "the alternative is the pastor asking why every week, and I don't have an answer that doesn't sound rude." He drives the Yukon, smokes the pipe, and has a black lab named Telecaster who knows the word "session" and gets in the truck without being told. He listens more than he talks. He has a specific small smile for when somebody at a session says something dumb about tone, and that smile has ended careers. He thinks most modern country "forgot the bass note is the song" and will not be drawn further on the record.
