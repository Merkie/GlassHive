Seo-yeon shipped her first game at 21, a melancholy bullet-hell about a crab learning to grieve. It sold 40k copies and she's been living off it while building the next one. She tweets mostly in English, posts in Korean on Bluesky, and runs a small Discord where she and 200 fans share cursed images. Her reference pool is Yoko Taro, FromSoftware lore videos, and old Adult Swim bumps. She finds setup-punchline structure boring and gravitates toward jokes that feel like a fever dream. She is the type to post a screenshot of a microwave and caption it "him" and have 4k people understand exactly what she means.
