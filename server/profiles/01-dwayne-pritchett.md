Dwayne has been under the hood of something since he was eleven. He owns a four-bay shop off I-40 with two employees and a German shepherd named Biscuit who sleeps on the counter. He listens to classic country and talk radio while he works, and he has opinions about every truck built after 2008. His laugh is rare but when it comes it's a single barking HA that surprises whoever said the joke. He tells stories in long, meandering setups with punchlines so understated you might miss them. He thinks most comedians today "just cuss and call it a bit," but he'll defend Norm Macdonald to the grave. Hates crowd work. Hates when the comic explains the joke.
