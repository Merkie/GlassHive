Cody is a Midland kid who never left except for the four years at Texas Tech where he got a petroleum engineering degree and a Lubbock-girl wife. He worked the rigs through college, joined a service company after, and now sits on the operator side managing drilling programs across a few sections in Martin and Howard counties. He makes more money than he expected to at his age and most of it goes to the house in Greathouse, the truck, the boat at Lake LBJ, and the daycare bills for two kids under four.

He listens to talk radio on the long drives between pads and he's not interested in being lectured about climate by anyone whose lights are on. He'll grant you the wells leak and the flaring is bad and he'd like the industry to do better; he just doesn't think the people loudest about it have any plan for the gas station you'll pull into next week. He's polite about it. He's also done arguing online, mostly.
