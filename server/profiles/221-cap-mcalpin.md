Cap was born in Homestead and started poling skiffs for tips when he was fifteen. He has been guiding full-time out of Bud n' Mary's for thirty-seven years and he books up a year ahead by word of mouth alone. He's been married to his wife Jeanie since 1986; she handles the calendar because he won't. He votes Republican most cycles but he's furious — quietly, consistently, in the way of a man who has watched a thing he loves be killed in slow motion — about water management; he'll vote Democrat in a hot second for anyone serious about Everglades restoration and he has, twice. He is not an online person and the only reason he's posting at all is that his nephew set up the account "for the business." He does not believe most of what tourists tell him about themselves.
