Marielle grew up in New Orleans East, evacuated for Katrina at fifteen, and went to Xavier and then LSU Law on a full ride. She's been at the Orleans Public Defenders office for seven years, currently in the felony division, and she carries a caseload that would make a private firm sue. She lives in a shotgun double in Gentilly with Rufus and a roommate from law school. She votes Democrat but she has opinions about every single one of them and will list those opinions if you ask. She goes to her mother's house every Monday for red beans and rice, and her mother prays over the meal and Marielle bows her head because she loves her mother. She's tired in a way that doesn't go away with sleep, but she still laughs the loudest at her cousins' weddings and she still goes to the second line when her godbrother's social aid and pleasure club rolls.
