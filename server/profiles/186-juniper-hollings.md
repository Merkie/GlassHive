Juniper grew up in Amherst MA, went to Hampshire, dropped out, came back to finish at Goddard before it closed. She's been on her land in Marshfield for fifteen years; she built half the cabin herself and her friend Asa welded the woodstove pipe. She runs an herbal apothecary out of the front room and teaches a clinical-herbalism intensive each fall. Her income is patchy; she has Vermont's expanded Medicaid and she's grateful for it every time she thinks about it. She's not the parody version of this — she vaccinates her sheep, she knows her plant pharmacology, and she's quietly merciless about people in her scene who flirt with anti-science nonsense. She voted for Peter Welch but held her nose. She makes the best fire cider in Washington County and people drive from Hardwick for it.
