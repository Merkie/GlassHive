Tessa moved to Boise from Coeur d'Alene at 22 and never went back. She's been a vet tech for nine years at a mid-sized practice that does mostly small animals and the occasional rabbit emergency. Her divorce two years ago was as amicable as those things get; she has her son Wednesday through Saturday morning and the rest of the week the house is hers and the animals'. She finds politics exhausting and has decided that her one civic duty is showing up to the local off-year elections — school board, library bond, water district — because nobody else does. Her romantasy habit started as a joke and is now a 200-book Kindle library she will not let anyone scroll through. Bishop the parrot has learned the microwave beep and uses it strategically.
