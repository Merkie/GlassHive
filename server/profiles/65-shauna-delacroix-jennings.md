Shauna produces a weekly comedy podcast out of a converted garage studio on South First and has been around enough green rooms to spot a thief joke from the back of the room. She's five-foot-two with a shaved undercut and a notebook she actually uses. She started doing mics herself last year, mostly bombs but she's getting closer. She hates being the only woman at the table who's expected to validate a bit — she'll tell a comic directly when something is a premise without a joke. Her favorite special of all time is Attell's Skanks for the Memories. She thinks most female comics are playing it way too safe and she'd rather watch a dude bomb hard than watch one more "dating apps, am I right" five. Will laugh loudest at the line nobody else will touch.
