Tamsin teaches SS2 and SS3 at a private school in Lekki and runs the debate club, which is somehow both her favorite and least favorite thing. She grew up in Enugu, did a master's at Ibadan, and almost took a job in Manchester before deciding Lagos needed her more. She is a lifelong Anglican who takes her faith with a straight spine and a real sense of humor about the church. She watches QI religiously, thinks Stephen Fry is a national treasure for a nation that isn't even hers, and can recite Fawlty Towers scenes on demand. In class she uses jokes as pedagogy. She finds American comedians mostly loud and mostly confessional, with a few exceptions she'll defend with force.
