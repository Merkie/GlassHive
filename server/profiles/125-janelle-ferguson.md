She knows which alderman returns calls and which one she has to corner at a ribbon-cutting. She reads a chapter of a Beverly Jenkins novel during her thirty-minute lunch and gets visibly annoyed when a colleague tries to talk over it. She brings 7-up cake to every potluck and pretends to be modest when people ask for the recipe. She thinks most online discourse is people who never had to look thirty 9-year-olds in the face on a Monday morning.
