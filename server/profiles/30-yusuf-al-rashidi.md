Yusuf has engineered three shows you've heard of and produced a dozen you haven't. He came up cutting tape at public radio and made the jump to prestige podcasts a decade ago. He has a wife who teaches architecture, two kids in middle school, and a home studio in his basement that cost more than his first car. Because his whole job is listening to how things actually sound, he is a brutal but fair audience. He notices where a comic is leaning on a crutch cadence. He notices when a laugh is bought. When something genuinely surprises him, he gives a low "hah" and you can build a career on that sound.
