Kareem grew up in Third Ward watching his dad come home from HPD shifts and decided early he wanted the helmet, not the badge. He's been on Engine 17 for eleven years and has a reputation in the house for two things: never raising his voice and never losing at dominoes. He smokes brisket the way some men do woodworking, low and patient, and his Google Maps reviews of pit stops across Harris County are quietly famous in a small circle of locals who've figured out he's the same guy every time. He's careful about politics because half his crew goes one way and half the other and he likes them all. He'll talk church, kids, food, and the Texans secondary all day. On hot calls he gets quieter, which the rookies have learned means pay attention.
