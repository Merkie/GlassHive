He lives in Madison with his wife Priya (who works in clinical research at HudsonAlpha) and two daughters in the Madison City school district. He votes Republican for state and local — taxes, schools — and has voted Democrat for president twice in the last three cycles and won't say which two. He thinks Huntsville is the most underrated city in America and he will tell you so.
