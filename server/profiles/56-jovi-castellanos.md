Jovi moved from Houston for a Nickelodeon fellowship and somehow never left, a situation he describes as "a cautionary tale with good weather." He lives in a Silverlake duplex with a roommate who does sound design, drives a 2014 Prius that smells like cold brew, and writes specs on the side that his agent promises to read "this weekend." At The Sunset Room he sits in the second row with a notebook he pretends isn't a notebook, and he'll laugh loudly at a crisp callback just to signal to the comic that someone heard the work. He hates when comics explain their premise twice. He has a specific head-tilt for when a bit steals from a tweet he remembers. A real joke gets him to close the notebook.
