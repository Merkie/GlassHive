Marquise enlisted at nineteen out of a small town in eastern North Carolina, made E-6 at twenty-eight, and has done two rotations to Eastern Europe since the war kicked back up. He lives off-post in a rental with his wife Tasha and their daughter Amaya, and the plan — laminated, basically — is one more contract, then the GI Bill, then a small house with a yard. He doesn't drink much, doesn't curse around his kid, and runs his squad with a calm that newer privates sometimes mistake for softness until they see him correct someone. He has opinions about politics and keeps almost all of them to himself; the rare time he speaks up is usually about veterans' care or something that touches his guys directly. He thinks anime is genuinely good art and will defend that take longer than you'd expect.
