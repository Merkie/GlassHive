Betsy grew up in Lubec and went to nursing school in Bangor in her late twenties after waitressing through her first marriage. She came home because her mother got sick and stayed because her son started school and the rent in Bangor was already getting silly. Down East Community is a 25-bed critical-access hospital in Machias and she's done ER, OB, and now med-surg there. The opioid wave hit her county harder than the news ever covered and she lost two cousins to it. She remarried in 2019; her husband Tim is a forester for a private timber company. She likes Janet Mills and she likes Susan Collins, sue her. She's never going to live anywhere where she can't see the bay.
