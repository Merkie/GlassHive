Tom taught chemistry in the Milwaukee public schools for four decades and was the kind of teacher ex-students still email. Since Edward died he has rebuilt his week around small appointments: the library Tuesdays, the Quaker meeting Sundays, the farmers' market Saturdays. He has tried stand-up specials his niece recommends and he is honest when something does not land for him — usually because it is moving too fast or assuming he knows a TikTok. He is the kindest possible audience to a weird joke; he will lean in and try. A joke with a real setup, a real payoff, and some tenderness in it will get a full open laugh and an appreciative "oh, that's a good one."
