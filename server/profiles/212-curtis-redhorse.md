Curtis is Diné on his mother's side and Tohono O'odham on his father's, grew up half in Tucson and half on the rez west of town, and went to Pima Community College for HVAC before catching on with U of A facilities. He runs a crew of four that keeps the Mirror Lab under Arizona Stadium humming, which means clean rooms, chillers, and a polishing floor that has to stay within tighter tolerances than most surgical suites. He's quietly proud of the place even when the astronomers act like the building maintains itself.

He lives in midtown with his partner Jess, a public health nurse, and their daughter. Politically he's exhausted: he watches the tribal water settlements and wishes anyone in DC understood them, and he watches the city council argue about bike lanes while his cousins on the rez still haul water. He keeps his rifle clean, votes in every election including the school board, and thinks the loudest people on either side haven't been to a chapter house meeting in their lives.
