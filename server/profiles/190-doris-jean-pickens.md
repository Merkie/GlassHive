Doris Jean has worked out of the Hale County extension office on Main Street since 1991. She teaches young mothers how to stretch a SNAP dollar across a week and how to keep collards from going bitter, and she runs the 4-H sewing club where most of her students are the granddaughters of her first students. She lost her husband Calvin to a stroke in 2019 and moved her oldest grandbaby in for a year while her daughter finished nursing school at Wallace State. She drives a 2014 Camry, keeps a casserole dish in the trunk in case somebody needs feeding, and does not suffer foolishness from the county commission. She votes Democrat the way she breathes air. She remembers the freedom riders coming through and her uncle being on the Greensboro voter rolls before most of her white classmates' parents were. She'll pray for you and tell you about yourself in the same breath.
