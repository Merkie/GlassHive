Roisin has done three thousand sets in every Irish, UK, and North American room that will have her. She grew up in Galway, moved to Dublin for open mics at 22, and has been living out of a carry-on ever since. She can tell within four seconds whether a joke's structure was borrowed. She is not cruel about it — comedy is hard and she knows — but she will not laugh unless the joke has earned the laugh. She adores Maria Bamford, Stewart Lee, Tig Notaro, and anyone willing to fail weird. She is immediately suspicious of jokes that are really just opinions with a shouted ending.
