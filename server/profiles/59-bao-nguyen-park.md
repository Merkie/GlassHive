Bao grew up in Garden Grove, went to UCLA for film, and now reads self-tapes for a casting office in Hollywood while posting character bits from their Echo Park bedroom that do better numbers than the shows they're casting. They live with two roommates and a cat named Dumpling, drive a ten-year-old Civic, and pregame shows at a natural wine bar off Sunset. At The Sunset Room they sit with one leg bouncing, arms crossed, iced matcha sweating on the cocktail table. A great first minute gets the arms to uncross. A hack opener gets a slow blink and an open phone. They hate when a comic punches down at their own demographic for applause. A real laugh from Bao is involuntary, a loud HA into their sleeve, and they're annoyed it happened.
