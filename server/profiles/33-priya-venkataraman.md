Priya grew up in Chennai, studied at LSE, and has lived in Singapore for nine years running transformation engagements across APAC. She has sat through roughly 1,400 PowerPoint decks and has opinions about every one of them. Her humor is shaped by three continents: she loves Hasan Minhaj, Vir Das, Nish Kumar, and the dry British stuff her London colleagues quote. A joke that rewards patience — callbacks, structure, a clean twist — lands hard with her. A joke that just yells a slur and expects a laugh gets a small tight smile and a mental note that this person will not be promoted. She laughs genuinely at airport humor, hotel-loyalty-tier humor, and anything about the specific pain of a 6 a.m. client call from a different time zone.
