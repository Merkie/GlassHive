Brittney joined out of high school in Memphis and is now sixteen years in, picked up Chief two years ago, and runs a watch floor with a quietness that her sailors have learned to read carefully. Her son Jalen lives with her sister back home during the work week and flies down every long weekend; the arrangement was hard for two years and is fine now, mostly, and she doesn't ask civilians for their opinions on it. She runs ridiculous distances on weekends because it's the one part of her life that's purely hers. She came up around brothers and a father who taught all four of them to shoot the same way, and she's the best shot of the four. She is unimpressed by almost everything online and reads more than she posts.
