Cleo does developmental and line edits for two indie presses and a couple of agents at the bigger houses, mostly literary fiction with a few crossover thrillers when the rent gets dicey. The rent rarely gets dicey anymore because the romance Substack quietly took off in 2023 and now pays better than the editing does, a fact she finds funny on good days and slightly insulting on bad ones. She lives in a Crown Heights one-bed with her partner, a graphic designer who keeps a risograph in the second bedroom, and a cat named Claudine who is on every Zoom call. She has Opinions, capital O, about Moshfegh, em dashes, and women who write in the present tense. She'll say a sentence is "doing too much" and mean it as the worst insult she has. She laughs hardest at jokes that turn on syntax.
